

# **Hands-on AI assignment**

## Background

As an AI specialist you were assigned to help Disney improve their customer experience and satisfaction. You are provided with a dataset of customer reviews about their parks. The dataset in here:  [Link to dataset](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1T-BaJYt6LekYc-du1X0i2eFmvzOv2Dy6/view?usp=drive_link)  
The dataset contains reviews about the customer experience and metadata such as dates, the park location, the reviewer country and more. The customer experience team is overwhelmed by the number of reviews and turns to AI to help improve the experience. 

## Task

1. Perform data analysis of the data to uncover **actionable** insights to improve customer experience. For each analysis you do, provide a recommendation of what action can be taken based on it.   
   1. The expectation is that the analysis will also be based on the text and not only on the metadata, as the text contains most of the information  
   2. You can use an LLM to process the texts  
   3. There are no clear instructions on what insights we are interested in. It is up to you, your problem understanding and your creativity to come up with actionable insights   
2. The customer experience team wants to be able to ask open ended, natural language questions about the data. Build a system that enables getting questions about the dataset. The system should use both the text data and the metadata.

Example questions: 

* **What do visitors from Australia say about Disneyland in HongKong?**  
* **Is spring a good time to visit Disneyland?**  
* **Is Disneyland California usually crowded in June?**  
* **Is the staff in Paris friendly?**

	Shortly explain how you would evaluate the quality of the solution.

3. Prepare a short presentation (can also be a notebook) that explains your work, your line of thought, challenges and tradeoffs and innovative ideas

## General instructions

We will assess candidates based on the following:

* **Data Intuition:** Your approach to the problem and how you utilize data to develop practical solutions.  
* **Data Creativity:** Your ability to employ data in novel ways to address challenges.  
* **LLM Proficiency:** Your competence in working with Large Language Models and your understanding of LLM technology.  
* **Coding Skills:** Your ability to produce clear and comprehensible code within a reasonable timeframe.  
* **Communication:** Your effectiveness in explaining your methodologies clearly.
